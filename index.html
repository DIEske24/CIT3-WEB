Welcome to In&Out

In&Out is an interactive and community driven sex education platform designed for Filipino teens, young adults, and parents. Our goal is to provide accessible, accurate, and stigma-free information about reproductive health, relationships, and consent. Through engaging lessons, fun quizzes, and a supportive forum, we empower users to make informed decisions about their well-being.
